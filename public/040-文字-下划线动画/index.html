Last year, I went to a resort for my vacation. The resort was beautiful, filled with trees and sparkly porous area. The rooms were spacious and had great light. The soft beds felt like lying on a cloud. The resort offered many activities, such as swimming, beach volleyball, and bonfire party. I also made many friends at the bonfire party. Overall, this trip to the resort was one of the best memories of my life, and I miss it dearly.
